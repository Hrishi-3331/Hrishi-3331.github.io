Abstract:

Tronix 2021 is a social platform for all the somophores of Electronics and Communication Engineering Department of VNIT Nagpur. Get everything related to your department at a single place. App is developed to with an aim of connecting all the second years of ECE department to a common channel.

Images:

Details:

Key features of this app are-

Forum / News-feed: Get updates about what's going on in your department. This section allows second years to post question for discussion or to add any general post. Users can comment on these posts, write answers to the questions posted on feed, view comments by other users and also view answers posted by other users. Post/Question can be uploaded along with image. Images can also be uploaded in comments / answers. Download any image from newsfeed by long pressing it! Additional features include polls and Birthday cards.

Study Section: It's really a tedious task to find assignments, previous year question papers, notes, and other study material during exam time. This section provides you with a common solution to all this. This section contains assignments, notes, practical references and previous year question papers of all subjects. Users can download them from here and then access it offline from here.This study material will get updated regularly on servers and when new content is available, it can be downloaded using same app.

Attendance Manager: Plan your bunks in a smart way! This section provides a personal attendance manager to all users. Users can keep a track of their attendance from here. This is a personal section and attendance recorded in this section won't be monitored by app server. No need to set your timetable, it would be automatically fetched by servers. Just mark whether you attended the class, or bunked it or class was cancelled!

Student Notices: Irritated by whatsapp notifications for all unnecessary messages on department group? Many times you miss important messages due to these unwanted messages. This section contains all these important messages / notices which you should not miss. Get notifications of these notices even when app is in background.

Classmates: Not saved contact numbers of all your classmates yet? No need to do it now! Call any of your classmate from here without saving or dialing his/her number. Get list of all registered second years in order of increasing enrollment no. and contact any of them directly from here.
