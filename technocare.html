Abstract:

Smart Phone are growing more and more powerful to carry out various complex tasks. We hereby propose an idea and prototype of smartphone app – “Technocare”. Main aim of this app is to help consumers to live a healthy life by regular monitoring of their daily activities. App also aims to increases patient engagement, help individuals to focus on improving their health and wellness by sticking to a healthy diet plan, regular hospital visits for checkups, and adhering to their medication schedule along with follow-up appointments.

Images:

Details:

Key features of this app are-

Paperless E-Prescription Service.

Hospital Visits record and online appointment booking service.

Sleep time tracker.

Details about various Hospitals in locality, health services provided by them.

Allow users to view and write reviews and ratings to various health centers in locality.

Health Feed that contains various articles on healthcare habits, diseases, exercises, healthy food, new technologies in medical science, etc to promote knowledge and create awareness.

Daily footsteps tracker and thereby calculating number of calories burnt.

Weather and air quality information updates of your region.

Emergency contact details of various health centers in your locality.
